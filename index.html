About Us

SDSP is a stripped-down, riff-driven, post-punk indie offshoot of Swim Dads. Loud, heavy, and fuzzy, the band leans into original material that blends raw energy with thick, groove-laden distortion.

It’s a space for experimentation, volume, and heavier influences - built for basement shows, late nights, and walls of sound.
